## File Formats for Video

### For Diagrams:
- **Tool**: Excalidraw (excalidraw.com) or draw.io
- **Style**: Clean, modern, consistent colors
- **Colors**:
  - Primary: `#00FFA3` (Solana green)
  - Secondary: `#9945FF` (Solana purple)
  - Background: `#1A1A2E` (Dark blue)
  - Text: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- **Export**: PNG @ 2x resolution

### For Terminal:
- **Font**: Fira Code or JetBrains Mono, 18pt
- **Theme**: Dracula or One Dark
- **Terminal**: iTerm2 or Alacritty
- **Recording**:asciinema or direct screen capture
